1. OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This Privacy Policy applies to the GMR Partner mobile application ("App"),
operated by GMR.

The GMR Partner app is designed for service providers and business partners
who list and manage their services on the Guide My Route platform.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the practices
described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use
the App.

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.
Your data is used solely to provide, improve, and manage our partner services.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2.1  Account & Identity Information
     - Full name
     - Email address (for login, notifications, and support)
     - Profile photo (optional)
     - Google account details (if you sign in via Google Sign-In)
     - Phone number (for partner contact and verification)

2.2  Partner & Business Information
     - Business name, type, and description
     - Services offered, operating hours, and pricing details
     - Business location and service areas
     - Identity verification documents (where required for onboarding)

2.3  Financial & Payout Information
     - Banking or payment details (for processing your earnings)
     - Transaction and payout history
     - Tax identification details (where applicable)

2.4  Performance & Operational Data
     - Booking counts and booking history
     - Customer ratings and reviews received
     - Response times and acceptance rates
     - Revenue and earnings metrics

2.5  Device & Usage Information
     - Device model, OS version, and unique device ID
     - Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token for push notifications
     - App usage analytics (screens visited, features used, session duration)
     - Crash reports and error logs
     - IP address (for security and fraud prevention)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We use the information we collect to:

  - Create and manage your partner account and verify your identity
  - List your services and make them discoverable to users
  - Process bookings, payments, and partner earnings
  - Send push notifications for new bookings and customer enquiries
  - Display your performance metrics and business analytics
  - Respond to support requests and resolve disputes
  - Monitor and analyse app usage to improve partner tools
  - Detect, investigate, and prevent fraud or illegal activities
  - Comply with applicable legal and financial regulations
  - Send service-related and (with your consent) promotional communications

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. INFORMATION SHARING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  Recipient            | What Is Shared                    | Purpose
  ---------------------|-----------------------------------|--------------------
  App Users            | Business name, services, ratings  | Service discovery
  Payment Processors   | Payout / banking details          | Process your earnings
  Service Providers    | Data needed to operate services   | Hosting, analytics
  Law Enforcement      | As required by law                | Legal compliance
  Business Transfers   | Partner data as part of assets    | Merger or acquisition

All service providers are contractually bound to use data only for the
specified purpose and to protect it with appropriate security measures.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The GMR Partner app integrates with the following third-party services:

  Service                     | Provider       | Purpose
  ----------------------------|----------------|------------------------------
  Firebase Authentication     | Google LLC     | User sign-in & sessions
  Firebase Cloud Messaging    | Google LLC     | Push notifications
  Google Sign-In              | Google LLC     | OAuth authentication
  Supabase                    | Supabase Inc.  | Database & backend API
  Expo Notifications          | Expo           | Push notification delivery

Each service has its own Privacy Policy. We are not responsible for the
privacy practices of these third-party providers.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6. PUSH NOTIFICATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We may send the following push notifications to partners:

  - New booking requests from users
  - Booking confirmations and cancellations
  - Customer messages and enquiries
  - Payout and earnings notifications
  - Promotional updates and platform announcements (with your consent)
  - Security alerts and account activity notifications

You can manage or disable notifications at any time through your device
settings or within the app. Disabling notifications may delay your response
to booking requests and affect your performance rating.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
7. DATA RETENTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Data Type                   | Retention Period
  ----------------------------|-------------------------------------------
  Account information         | Duration of account + 90 days after deletion
  Booking history             | 3 years from the date of the booking
  Partner earnings records    | 7 years (for financial/tax compliance)
  Identity verification docs  | Duration of partnership + as required by law
  Crash & diagnostic logs     | 90 days
  Push notification tokens    | Until account deletion or token refresh
  Legal / compliance records  | As required by applicable law

When you close your partner account, we will delete or anonymise your
personal data within 90 days, except where retention is required by law
(e.g., financial records must be kept for 7 years).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
8. YOUR RIGHTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the following rights:

  - Access        : Request a copy of your personal data
  - Rectification : Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  - Erasure       : Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  - Portability   : Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  - Restriction   : Request limits on how we process your data
  - Objection     : Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  - Withdraw Consent : Revoke consent at any time where applicable

NOTE: Some data (e.g., financial transaction records) may not be erasable
due to legal retention requirements.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us (see Section 12).
We will respond within 30 days.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The GMR Partner app is intended for adults and registered business entities
only. It is NOT intended for children under the age of 18.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we discover
we have inadvertently collected such data, we will promptly delete it and
deactivate the associated account.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
10. DATA SECURITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  - Encryption in transit via TLS/HTTPS for all data transmissions
  - Secure authentication via OAuth 2.0 (Google Sign-In + Firebase Auth)
  - Access controls — only authorised personnel can access personal data
  - Supabase Row-Level Security (RLS) for database-level protection
  - Encrypted storage of sensitive financial and identity data
  - Regular security audits of our infrastructure and codebase

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you
suspect a security breach or unauthorised account access, please contact
us immediately and change your password.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
11. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material
changes, we will:

  - Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document
  - Send a push notification to all partners (where feasible)
  - Display a prominent in-app notice when you next open the app

Continued use of the app after the effective date constitutes acceptance
of the updated policy.
